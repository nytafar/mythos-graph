# Eliade vs marketing-side — camouflaged sacred at the marketplace

## The disagreement

Eliade's diagnosis of camouflaged-sacred IS the diagnosis of
marketing-side mythos. The closing chapter names the pattern at
maximum sharpness: modern man as *descendant of homo religiosus*
([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p270]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p271]])
who carries "a large stock of camouflaged myths and degenerated
rituals" ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p272]]). The examples Eliade
gives are surgical: Marx's classless society as eschatological
myth ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p273]]) — secular eschatology
with primordial age (the "golden age" of pre-class society),
present-age ordeal (alienation under capital), and final
restoration (classless communism); psychoanalysis as desacralized
initiatory descent ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p274]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p275]])
— the descent into unconscious depths, the encounter with the
primordial other, the integration as return; modern professional
struggle and ordeal-narrative ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p275]])
— the trial, the threshold, the transformation, all run as
secular sacred without the cosmology that would make the language
true.

The marketing-side compression that the engine works against —
Miller's StoryBrand 7-frame, Sinek's "Start with Why," Godin's
permission/tribe vocabulary — is precisely Eliade's camouflaged-
sacred operating in the marketplace. The marketing-side endpoint
adopts mythic vocabulary (transformation, journey, threshold,
ritual, sacred, tribe, the One, hero, guide, villain) without the
corresponding cosmological commitment. The brand performs sacred
— claims to transform, to initiate, to consecrate the customer's
life — without doing the sacred's work. The vocabulary is real;
the cosmology underneath is missing.

The marketing-side counterparty is composite — not one source, but
a frame-family. Miller (StoryBrand: 7-frame compression of Campbell
into brand-customer template), Sinek (Start with Why: WHY/HOW/WHAT
golden-circle, "people don't buy what you do, they buy why you do
it"), Godin (Tribes, Purple Cow, permission marketing). The
compression is shared structurally even where the surface
vocabulary differs.

## Why operative

This is the corpus's central anti-pattern made visible as a
tension. Eliade names what marketing-side does; the marketing-side
endpoint exists *because* desacralization left the vocabulary of
sacred unmoored from cosmology, and the marketplace picked up the
unmoored vocabulary and put it to work.

The tension is operative-for-the-engine in a specific way. Engine
canon writes brand-mythos using sacred vocabulary — sacred space,
sacred time, ritual, threshold, hierophany, axis-mundi, paradigmatic
model. Every move that adopts the vocabulary without doing the
corresponding cosmological work *becomes* camouflaged-sacred.
Engine discipline exists to do the sacred's work, not just perform
its language. The tension surfaces every time the workshop satellite
must check whether a brand-canon move is *carrying its cosmology*
or merely *carrying its vocabulary*.

The corrective is not to drop the vocabulary — that surrenders the
territory to the marketing-side endpoint. The corrective is to do
the cosmological work the vocabulary names. A brand that names a
"threshold" must specify the cosmology in which threshold is real
(break-in-plane, ontological-status-change, the world's
nonhomogeneity). A brand that names "transformation" must specify
what *was* and what *is*, with the same ontological weight Eliade
gives initiation-as-cosmogony ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p262]]).
A brand that names "ritual" must specify the rite's structure —
preliminal-liminal-postliminal, separation-incorporation, the rite
as the *operative mechanism* the cosmology runs through.

This is the engine's mission stated as a tension: the brand-mythos
the engine builds is the *opposite* of marketing-side compression,
and the discipline that distinguishes them is doing-cosmology vs.
performing-cosmological-vocabulary.

## Pressure-test signature

Every brand-mythos move that adopts mythic vocabulary —
transformation, journey, threshold, ritual, sacred, tribe, hero,
guide, villain, boon, return — without the corresponding
cosmological commitment IS camouflaged-sacred in Eliade's sense.
The corrective is not to drop the vocabulary but to do the
cosmological work the vocabulary names — i.e., the engine's
mission.

Concrete check: for each piece of mythic vocabulary in canon,
where does the canon name the cosmological commitment that makes
the vocabulary true?

- "Threshold" — what break-in-plane, what ontological-status-
  change, what before-and-after with operative weight?
- "Transformation" — what was, what is, what mechanism enacted the
  change, what does the changed-self carry that the prior-self did
  not?
- "Ritual" — what preliminal, what liminal, what postliminal? what
  separation, what incorporation? what is the rite's mechanism?
- "Sacred" — sacred-as-relation or sacred-as-quality (see
  [[van-gennep-vs-eliade-sacred-as-relation-vs-quality]])? what
  configuration or what hierophany?
- "Hero" / "guide" / "villain" — what archetypal commitment, what
  shadow form, what cosmological position?
- "Journey" — what twelve-stage or three-phase structure, what
  customer-arc with named stages, what return-with-elixir?

This tension surfaces at controlling-idea (whether the central
moral claim carries cosmological weight or only mythic vocabulary),
villain (whether the antagonist is moral force or marketing-side
strawman), guide (whether the brand-voice carries authority or
performs authority), and boon (whether transformation reaches
identity-or-relational layer or stays at functional with mythic
language).

## Resolution direction

Eliade wins as diagnosis; the marketing-side endpoint is the
failure mode he names. The resolution is not "both stand" — the
marketing-side compression is what Eliade reads as camouflaged-
sacred, and the engine's mission is to unmask the camouflage by
doing the cosmological work.

But the resolution is also not "abandon the vocabulary." The
engine takes the vocabulary back from the marketing-side endpoint
by doing the sacred's actual work in the brand-mythos canon. The
vocabulary is the engine's territory; the marketing-side has
been performing it without backing it; the engine builds canon
that backs the vocabulary.

This is the engine's *standing tension* — operative not at one
canon-move but at every canon-move where mythic vocabulary
appears. The pressure-test signature fires constantly, and the
resolution-direction is the engine's discipline.

The tension cross-references the anti-patterns:
[[camouflaged-sacred-in-modernity]] carries the operative-
diagnostic load (the failure mode workshop satellites fire on);
this tension carries the methodological-genealogy load (the
foundational-primary diagnosis from which the anti-pattern
descends). Both kept, with cross-reference per the
tension-vs-anti-pattern role-split discipline.

## Contributions

### From [[eliade-sacred-and-profane|The Sacred and the Profane]]

Eliade names the camouflaged-sacred at maximum sharpness in Ch IV.
Modern man as descendant of homo religiosus at
[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p270]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p271]];
the camouflaged stock at [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p272]]:
"modern man, who feels and claims that he is nonreligious, still
retains a large stock of camouflaged myths and degenerated
rituals." The Marx-eschatology gloss at
[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p273]] — "Marx's classless society
and the consequent disappearance of all historical tensions find
their most exact precedent in the myth of the Golden Age which
many traditions put at the beginning and the end of history" —
names a secular myth running on borrowed sacred-time
infrastructure. The psychoanalysis-as-initiation reading at
[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p274]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p275]]
names the inner-descent as desacralized version of initiatory-
death-and-rebirth, which is what marketing-side "transformational
journey" language inherits two-removes-down. The professional-
struggle-as-ordeal observation at [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p275]]
runs the same compression: ordeal-language without the rite-
structure that would make the ordeal initiatory.

The marketing-side counterparty is composite. Miller, Sinek,
Godin, and the broader downstream (Vogler at the screenwriter
endpoint of Campbell-compression; Mark and Pearson at the brand-
applied-archetypes endpoint of Pearson-compression) all share the
structural move Eliade names — adopt the sacred's vocabulary, drop
the sacred's cosmology, run the vocabulary as marketing
infrastructure. Each individual marketing-side source will gain
its own per-source treatment as it is read; the composite-tension
holds the family-level disagreement at full operative weight
because Eliade's side is fully anchored and the marketing-side's
shared structural move (mythic vocabulary without cosmological
commitment) is observable across the family without requiring
each member's source-read.

The tension is `operative_for_pressure_test: true` at composite
counterparty status because the engine's standing discipline
*is* this pressure-test — every mythic-vocabulary canon move runs
it. The composite frame does not gate the operative-firing; the
firing condition is "mythic vocabulary appears in canon," and the
check is "is the cosmological commitment present."
