# Eliadean-sacred sphere

## Frame commitments

- **Sacred and profane as two modes of being.** Not domains, not
  zones, not value-pairs — two existential situations the human
  inhabits in the world. The distinction is ontological before it
  is classificatory ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p011]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p012]]).
- **Hierophany as ontological founding.** The sacred manifests; the
  manifestation founds a world. "The manifestation of the sacred
  ontologically founds the world"
  ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p020]]). Sacred is *encountered*,
  not *constructed*. The brand or culture that says "we make this
  sacred" is making a claim Eliade's frame rules out a priori.
- **Axis mundi as cosmic structure.** The break in space, the
  opening between cosmic regions, the vertical connecting form
  (pillar, mountain, tree, ladder), the Centre that proliferates
  — the four-part schema at
  [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p044]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p045]]
  is what makes "world" possible at all in this frame.
- **Illud tempus as recoverable primordial time.** Sacred time is
  not historical past; it is the founding instant ritually made
  present again. The festival reactualises; the participant
  becomes contemporary with the gods
  ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p085]],
  [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p119]]).
- **Myth as paradigmatic model.** What the gods did *in illo
  tempore* is the pattern every meaningful human act imitates.
  Acts without paradigmatic model are "vain and illusory…
  unreal" ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p131]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p134]]).
  Imitatio dei as ontological route to becoming.
- **Deus otiosus as structural pattern.** Celestial supreme beings
  withdraw; specialists in procreation take over the cult; the
  high god is invoked only in extremity. Pattern recurs across
  substrates ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p168]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p177]]).
- **Camouflaged sacred as diagnosis of modernity.** Religious
  patterns persist inside avowedly secular forms — new-year ritual,
  career-as-initiation, psychoanalysis-as-descent, political
  eschatology — the structure does its work without recognition
  ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p272]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p275]]).
- **Nostalgia for paradise as religious anthropology of desire.**
  Religious man wants to live in the world "fresh, pure, and
  strong," as it came from the Creator's hands. The longing is
  structural, not affective ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p124]]–[[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p126]]).
- **"Religious man thirsts for being."** The single-line anchor
  ([[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p081]]). The pull toward sacrality
  is the pull toward the real. Profane in its pure form approaches
  "absolute non being." The frame is not neutral on the question.

## Validation moves

When pressure-testing canon against this sphere:

- Trace any sacred-claiming canon back to a hierophanic anchor.
  What thing manifests the sacred? Where? On what occasion? If no
  anchor, the sacred-claim is unearned — strip it or reground.
- Check temporal claims for illud-tempus discipline. Does the
  canon invoke a founding instant the brand returns to, or is it
  performing timelessness without doing the reactualisation work?
- Test for paradigmatic-model shape on customer-arc and boon work.
  Does the brand reveal a paradigm the customer can imitate to
  become real, or only an outcome the customer can purchase?
- Test reductive moves. Does the canon reduce a sacred operation
  to a psychological need, a sociological function, a
  neurological state? Eliade's frame refuses reduction; canon that
  performs his vocabulary while reducing his ontology is failing
  the sphere.
- Check for camouflaged-sacred honesty. If the brand uses
  ritual-shaped copy with a desacralized audience, is it honoring
  the camouflage (doing the structure's work) or exploiting it
  (performing the language without the substrate)?
- Test cosmology against the four-part schema of
  [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p045]]: break in homogeneity,
  opening between regions, vertical connecting form, Centre. A
  cosmology missing one of the four is not yet operative in this
  frame.

## Failure symptoms

Canon failing this sphere will:

- Use sacred-flavoured language without naming what manifests the
  sacred (hierophany failure)
- Perform "timeless" tone without doing illud-tempus work (sacred
  time failure)
- Treat the brand's founding as biography rather than cosmogony
  (paradigmatic-model failure)
- Reduce sacred operations to "what they really are" psychologically
  or sociologically (Durkheim/Freud failure pattern)
- Borrow ritual structures while disclaiming their cosmology
  (camouflaged-sacred-as-exploitation, the operative anti-pattern)
- Treat axis-mundi as metaphor for organisational hierarchy rather
  than cosmological structure
- Promise paradise-restoration in the absence of any account of
  what fell

## Correctives

When failure fires:

- Anchor the sacred-claim to a specific hierophanic encounter the
  brand can stand by. If no anchor exists, the canon is making an
  unearned sacred claim — strip it or reground in a sphere the
  canon can hold (often phenomenological-without-sacred, or
  jungian-analytical with symbolic weight rather than ontological
  weight).
- For illud-tempus failures: locate the founding instant, write
  the cosmogony, name what every subsequent brand-act repeats.
- For paradigmatic-model failures: name the pattern the brand
  reveals, distinguish it from advice/feature/benefit, embody it
  in the brand-as-guide.
- For camouflaged-sacred-as-exploitation: either do the work the
  ritual structure implies, or strip the ritual language and
  rewrite in plainer register.

## What this sphere centers, touches, refuses

**Centers.** The cosmological-phenomenological frame: a sacred-
profane ontology read off the structure of religious experience
across substrates, with hierophany / axis mundi / illud tempus /
paradigmatic model as the operative grammar.

**Adjacent-touches.**

- [[jungian-analytical]] — Jung's archetype-as-substrate parallels
  Eliade's hierophany-as-substrate from inside the psyche.
  The two frames diagnose the same loss at the
  [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p243]] closing; flagged tension
  `eliade-vs-jung-cosmology-vs-psyche` is the seam.
- [[meaning-emergence]] — hierophany-as-revelation reads naturally
  against emergence frames; both refuse pure construction without
  collapsing into supernaturalism.
- [[pageau-symbolic]] — both treat symbol as ontological cipher,
  though Pageau re-mythologizes within Christian frame where
  Eliade reads structurally across.
- [[indo-european-substrate]] — Eliade draws comparatively from
  IE traditions; the sphere is the comparative substrate-set, this
  one is the methodological lens.

**Unaligned-with.**

- [[anglo-protestant-frame]] — Anglo-Protestant history-as-sacred
  reading (linear redemption-history, conscience-as-tribunal,
  individual-account theology) sits opposite Eliade's universalism
  of structure across substrates. Eliade reads Christianity at
  [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p089]] as one substrate's
  historicising of a structure that is universal; the
  Anglo-Protestant frame reads Christianity as the rupture that
  abolishes the structure. Operative cleavage.
- [[Girardian]] — Girard's mimesis-as-engine of culture is
  rivalrous and scapegoat-driven; Eliade's imitatio dei is
  generative and salutary. The two read foundational human
  imitation from opposite valuations. Both stand; they cannot be
  combined into a single frame without one swallowing the other.
- [[Norwegian-folkloric]] — Eliade is universal-claiming; the
  Norwegian substrate is parallel infrastructure, not flavour on
  the universal. The substrate is in productive tension with this
  sphere, not aligned with it. The substrate-touchpoint section
  carries the operative material.

## Norwegian-substrate touchpoint

The substrate confirms much of Eliade's frame at substrate scale —
hierophany at seter, gravhaug, hellige steiner; sacred-space at
gard-utgard; sacred-time at the calendar (jul, jonsok, olsok, 17.
mai); imago mundi at the langhus / stove / gard. These are
section augmentations, not breaks.

The substrate breaks Eliade sharply at three points, all
cosmological:

1. **Yggdrasil-as-gnawed-axis.** Eliade names Yggdrasil at
   [[eliade-sacred-and-profane#^p207]] as a cosmic-tree exemplar;
   the substrate's Yggdrasil is gnawed by Niðhǫggr at the root,
   browsed by deer, attacked across cosmic levels, eschatologically
   anticipating Ragnarǫk. Eliade's stable axis cannot describe
   this; the substrate operates a *stewarded-presence* cosmology
   the universal frame does not predict. Sharpest single break the
   corpus carries.
2. **Ragnarǫk vs eternal return.** Eliade's eternal return is
   reversible, recoverable, "indefinitely repeatable." Norse
   cosmology terminates at Ragnarǫk; the post-Ragnarǫk Gimli is
   not the same world recovered but a new world after irreversible
   end. Norse eschatological time is forward-pointing, not cyclic-
   recoverable.
3. **Ætt-individuation as paradigm-source.** Eliade's paradigmatic
   model is imitatio dei (gods, culture heroes, mythical
   ancestors). The substrate's paradigm is imitatio of one's own
   ætt — the lineage carrying the line forward — not of the gods.
   This is operative against Pearson's individuation as well; see
   [[ætt-individuation]].

The substrate augments Eliade where the frame can tolerate the
extension: at allemannsretten (sacred-space-as-shared-resource
where Eliade's individual-encounter framing is thin), at dugnad
(sacred-time-as-shared-labour where Eliade lacks the communal-
productive register), at the displaced-deus-otiosus pattern
surviving in oaths and saying-language without cult (Týr's day,
the Christian-overwritten place-names).

The substrate's stewardship-against-hostile-forces dimension is
the load-bearing augmentation: where Eliade's sacred is *given*
and *received*, the substrate's sacred is *held* — the gard against
utgard, Yggdrasil against Niðhǫggr, the ætt against time. Brands
working in cosmology dimension inherit two cosmologies and the
choice is operative.
