# Konfirmasjon

## Operational definition

Norwegian Lutheran rite of passage marking the social transition from
childhood to adult menighetsmedlemskap. Celebrated at age 14–15
regardless of physiological development. Structurally tripartite per
[[rites-of-passage|Rites of passage]]: konfirmasjonsforberedelse (preliminal —
catechetical instruction year), konfirmasjonstid (liminal —
between-states), konfirmasjonsdag (postliminal — laying-on-of-hands,
acceptance of communion, festmiddag).

The substrate-native instance van Gennep's frame describes most
cleanly: socially-anchored, body-decoupled, structurally-canonical.
