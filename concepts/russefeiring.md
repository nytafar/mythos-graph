# Russefeiring

## Operational definition

Late-modern Norwegian liminal phase marking the social passage of
graduating videregående. Russetiden runs from spring of the
avgangsår to 17. mai. Carries every diagnostic property of van
Gennep's liminal — visible mark (red overall — *russedress*),
suspended ordinary social rules, special speech and practices
(russenavn, russekort, russerop), public recognition of the
between-state, ritual closure on 17. mai with national
reincorporation.

Substrate-native demonstration of van Gennep's autonomous-liminal
claim [[van-gennep-rites-of-passage#^p376]] — the russetid is its
own state with its own rites, nested inside the broader
adolescence-to-adulthood passage.

Cross-tradition note on [[camouflaged-sacred]]: russefeiring is
Eliade-diagnosable at maximum sharpness as
*camouflaged-sacred-degraded-rite* — visible mark (russedress),
name-change (russenavn), customary suspension of social rules,
ritual closure on 17. mai. The Lutheran-substrate
refusal-of-camouflage paradox makes this *more* visible than
analogous secular-rites in ritually-saturated substrates;
brands working in Norwegian register can name russefeiring as
ritual openly without the audience reading the naming as
cynical. See [[camouflaged-sacred]] substrate section.
