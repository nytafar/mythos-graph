# Dugnad

## Operational definition

Norwegian collective volunteer work for shared benefit. The shared
task itself performs the rite-of-incorporation function for task-
defined micro-communities (borettslag, idrettslag, school class,
neighbourhood). Closes with mat (kaffe og kaker, pølser, sometimes a
larger felles måltid).

Augments van Gennep's postliminal-as-shared-meal with
postliminal-as-shared-work: the work is the rite, the meal is the
closure of the rite. The bond produced is rank-suspended (employer
and employee paint the school fence side by side) and persists in
the recognition that "we did this together." Husmannsånd-
egalitarian reading of the substrate.

Operative for brand-application: collaborative-effort as
incorporation rite, not just shared-ceremony.
